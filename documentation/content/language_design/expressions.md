## Arithmetic

- Addition `+`
- Subtraction `-`
- Division `/`

## Comparison

- Equality `==`
- Non equality `!=`
- Less than `<`
- More than `>`
- Less than or equal to `<=`
- More than or equal to `>=`

## Logical

- And `&`
- Or `|`
- Not `!`

## Other

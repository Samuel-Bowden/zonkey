## Arithmetic

- Addition `+`
- Subtraction `-`
- Division `/`
- Multiplication `*`

## Comparison

- Equality `==`
- Non equality `!=`
- Less than `<`
- More than `>`
- Less than or equal to `<=`
- More than or equal to `>=`

## Logical

- And `and` or `&`
- Or `or` or `|`
- Not `not` or `!`
